About AutoShop

Autoshop is a photo-editing tool that makes use of cutting edge deep learning techniques to add objects to and remove objects from images. The tool is innovative in the sense that there is no commercial product that can perform context-aware addition and removal of objects. The task of context-aware object addition and removal is challenging since the texture, opacity, brightness and other photographical details of the added object should match those of the background image. Currently, this process is handled by professional photoshoppers and it is quite time-consuming. Hence, Autoshop will allow people to edit their photos effortlessly.

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT ?

AutoShop aims to improve the grape producers experience and knowledge with grape related diseases.

Comprehensive features will help the users to keep an eye on their farms.

Detecting diseases will improve efficiency and quality by adding value to fresh and healthy grapes.

WHAT IS AUTOSHOP ?

AutoShop is a mobile application for people who are or interested in grape production.

Users are able to detect their grape’s disease by uploading a leaf image or answering diagnosis questionnaire.

Users can add their farms and learn the weather details or nearby diseases to be aware of any threat.

Core Features

Upload a photo of the grape leaf and identify its disease
By answering the diagnosis questionnarre, detect local diseases

Add the vineyard location to see the diseases occuring nearby
Be notified about the real-time local weather conditions

Learn treatment options and disease details
Monitor vineyards and weather conditions to prevent diseases
